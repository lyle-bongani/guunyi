A B O U T   T H E
P L A C E

Guunyi Homestead is a modern 4-bedroom rural haven in Bromley, Zimbabwe. Enjoy a peaceful, gated property with open-plan living, dedicated workspace, and all modern comforts. Savor farm-fresh eggs, indulge in farm-raised meats, and explore the countryside.

From sunrise through the indigenous trees to starlit evenings with a braai in hand, everything here is crafted to make you feel outside of time. Whether you come to unplug, to create, or simply to breathe deeper, this refuge meets you where you are.

Entire home · 4 guests · 4 bedrooms · 6 beds · 2 baths

Modern Comfort

Open-plan living, 4 bedrooms, dedicated workspace, 24/7 power and modern amenities throughout.

Farm & Food

Fresh organic vegetables, farm-fresh eggs, and farm-raised beef, fish, rabbit, and guinea fowl.

Nature Escape

Country walks, stargazing, swim in the dam, and explore the surrounding Zimbabwean countryside.
